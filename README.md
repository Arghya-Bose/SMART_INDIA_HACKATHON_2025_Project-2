<h2>IDEA TITLE</h2>

<h3>Proposed Solution</h3>
<ul>
  <li>QR-coded Blockchain ID for crop tracking → Farmers enter crop details during harvest; blockchain assigns unique batch ID.</li>
  <li>Records every transaction → Farm → Distributor → Retailer → Consumer.</li>
  <li>End-to-end supply chain tracking via blockchain logs.</li>
  <li>Real-time monitoring → Govt/NGOs detect fraud & ensure fair pricing.</li>
  <li>Consumers scan QR → See crop origin, harvest date, quality & full history.</li>
</ul>

<h3>How It Solves the Problem</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Fair Prices → No middlemen cheating.</li>
  <li>Transparency → QR shows origin, price, quality.</li>
  <li>Smart Contracts → Prevent overcharging.</li>
  <li>Government Monitoring → Live tracking & fraud detection.</li>
  <li>Trust Through Blockchain + Smart Contracts + QR.</li>
  <li>Low Cost → Works on smartphones, kiosks, cloud.</li>
  <li>Full traceability from farm to consumer.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Unique Value:</b> “Know Your Crop, Know Your Farmer.”</p>

<h2>TECHNICAL APPROACH</h2>

<h3>1️⃣ Programming & Frameworks</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Solidity → Smart Contracts</li>
  <li>React.js → Frontend & Backend</li>
  <li>Kotlin & XML → Mobile App</li>
  <li>Oracle → Real-time market prices</li>
</ul>

<h3>Technology</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Farmers add crop details (type, quantity, quality, location) → blockchain generates unique ID.</li>
  <li>Distributor updates transfer info → validated by smart contract.</li>
  <li>Retailer enters selling price → smart contract checks government rules.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Methodology</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Consumer scans QR → sees full crop journey & price history.</li>
  <li>Govt/NGOs monitor supply chain live → detect fraud instantly.</li>
</ul>

<h3>2️⃣ Blockchain & Smart Contracts</h3>

</ul>

<h3>3️⃣ Security Layer</h3>
